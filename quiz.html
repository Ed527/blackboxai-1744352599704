Which of the following best describes Newton's Second Law of Motion?

A An object at rest stays at rest unless acted upon by a force
B Force equals mass times acceleration (F = ma)
C For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction
D Energy cannot be created or destroyed

Explanation

Newton's Second Law of Motion states that the acceleration of an object is directly proportional to the net force acting upon it and inversely proportional to its mass. This is mathematically expressed as F = ma, where F is force, m is mass, and a is acceleration.
